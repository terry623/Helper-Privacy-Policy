Privacy Policy for Dcard Bilanx Helper

Last updated: 2023/10/24

Thank you for choosing Dcard Bilanx Helper. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will help you understand how we collect, use, and share your user data.

1. Data Collection

Our extension, Dcard Bilanx Helper, performs network monitoring to listen for specific tracking requests. The data we collect is limited to the payload of these tracking requests.

2. Data Usage

The data collected by Dcard Bilanx Helper is used solely for displaying on the GUI (Graphical User Interface) of the extension. We do not use this data for any other purpose, and it is not processed, analyzed, or stored for any secondary purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share the data collected by Dcard Bilanx Helper with any third parties. All data remains local to your browser and is not transmitted to any external servers or entities.

4. Data Storage

The data collected by Dcard Bilanx Helper is temporarily stored locally on your device for the sole purpose of displaying it on the GUI. It is not stored long-term, and any retained data is deleted when you close the extension or your browser.

5. Accessing and Controlling Your Data

As our extension does not transmit or store data externally, you have full control over your data. You can view the data at any time through the extension's GUI, and closing the extension or your browser will delete any temporarily stored data.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review our Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at terry@dcard.cc.
